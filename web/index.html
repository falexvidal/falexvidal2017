About

My name is Francisco Alexandre, also known as (F)alex. I’m a Digital Designer born in Brazil, but currently based in Toronto, ON, Canada.

I’m currently attending Interactive Media Design program at Seneca – York college campus, and my goal is having new experiences in Toronto, which include meeting new people, starting new partnerships and maybe finding my own voice here.

Living in a city like Toronto is like a dream come true. There is a massive design scene here, as well as lots of spaces and events developed by – and for designers.

I hope to make a difference here and help contributing to make Design not just a visual tool, but something that conveys meaning and delightful experiences to people.
